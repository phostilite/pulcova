What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide a better user experience.

Important to Know

Cookies cannot harm your device or files. They don't contain viruses, malware, or personal information like your name or address unless you specifically provide that information to us.

How We Use Cookies

Essential Functionality

  • • Website session management
  • • Security and CSRF protection
  • • Contact form functionality
  • • Dark/light theme preferences
  • • Language and region settings

Performance Enhancement

  • • Faster page loading
  • • Optimized user experience
  • • Reduced server load
  • • Cached static resources
  • • Improved website responsiveness

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Session Cookies

Purpose: Enable basic website functionality and security

Duration: Session-based (deleted when browser closes)

Can be disabled: No (required for website operation)

sessionid, csrftoken, django_language

2. Preference Cookies

Purpose: Remember your settings and preferences

Duration: Up to 1 year

Can be disabled: Yes (may affect user experience)

theme_preference, font_size, layout_mode

3. Third-Party Service Cookies

Google Fonts

Used to load custom fonts for better typography. Google may set cookies to optimize font delivery.

Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

CDN Services (Flowbite, Tailwind)

Content delivery networks may set cookies to optimize asset delivery and performance.

These services help improve website loading speed and performance.

Cookie Control and Your Rights

Your Control Options

You have full control over cookies on our website. You can accept, reject, or manage them according to your preferences.

Browser Settings

Chrome

Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data

Firefox

Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

What You Can Do

  • Block all cookies (may break website functionality)
  • Allow only essential cookies
  • Block third-party cookies only
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set up automatic cookie deletion

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Important Notice

Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website. Some features may not work properly without cookies.

If Essential Cookies Are Disabled

  • • Contact forms may not work
  • • Security features may be compromised
  • • Website functionality may break
  • • Session management will fail
  • • CSRF protection won't work

If Preference Cookies Are Disabled

  • • Theme preferences won't be saved
  • • Language settings will reset
  • • Layout preferences will be lost
  • • Customizations won't persist
  • • User experience may be reduced
